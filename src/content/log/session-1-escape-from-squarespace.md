After months of context building for my digital twin, I asked the AI to review my personal portfolio website and assess how it supports my career journey. I suddenly realized that I needed to plan my escape from Squarespace. Squarespace had served me for a number of years — before that Weebly, and before that WordPress. What I set out to build in its place serves to document my journey in real time as a Learning Engineer, by allowing me to design-in-public.

Session 1 is live. The scaffold is up: Astro 7 with React islands, strict TypeScript, static output, security hooks active, and a public repo at [GPayne12/georgemitchellpayne-com](https://github.com/GPayne12/georgemitchellpayne-com). Now deployed to Cloudflare Pages at [georgemitchellpayne.com](https://georgemitchellpayne.com).
